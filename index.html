Restaurant Introduction

A contemporary Melbourne dining room inspired by markets, laneways, and local growers.

Federation Lane serves modern Australian food with Mediterranean touches in the heart of Melbourne. The kitchen builds each menu around fresh seafood, Victorian lamb, local vegetables, handmade pasta, native herbs, and carefully selected wines.

Our dining room is relaxed enough for weeknight meals and polished enough for anniversaries, private dinners, pre-theatre bookings, and business lunches. Every table is looked after with genuine Melbourne hospitality.

Why guests visit

  • Seasonal produce sourced from Victoria
  • Vegetarian, seafood, and gluten-aware options
