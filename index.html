Restaurant Introduction

A Docklands dining room for noodles, dumplings, rice bowls, and casual night-time comfort food.

Welcome Restaurant, known in Chinese as 夜宵, brings a comforting menu of noodle soups, tossed noodles, rice dishes, dumplings, and savoury snacks to Victoria Harbour in Docklands.

Drop in for a quick lunch, settle in for dinner, or share dumplings and snacks with friends after work. The menu is bilingual, approachable, and built around hearty flavours.
